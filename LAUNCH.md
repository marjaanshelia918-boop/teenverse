# 🚀 TeenVerse Launch Guide

## Instant Launch in 3 Steps

### **Step 1: Open Settings**
Visit: https://github.com/marjaanshelia918-boop/teenverse/settings

### **Step 2: Enable GitHub Pages**
1. Click **"Pages"** in the left sidebar
2. Under "Build and deployment":
   - **Source:** Select `Deploy from a branch`
   - **Branch:** Select `main`
   - **Folder:** Select `/ (root)`
3. Click **Save**

### **Step 3: Get Your Live URL**
Your TeenVerse site will be live at:
```
https://marjaanshelia918-boop.github.io/teenverse/
```

⏱️ **Deployment time:** 1-2 minutes
